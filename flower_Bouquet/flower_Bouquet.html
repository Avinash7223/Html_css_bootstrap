Flowers Bouquet

Gardens and flowers have a way of bringing people together, drawing them from their homes

Favourite Flowers

Roses

The best thing about using this flower in making bouquets is that the different colors of roses behold different meanings which make expressing feelings easer.

Peony

The different shades of peonies are used in flower shops to create attractive bouquets to make you express your heartfelt feelings in a unique way.

Gerbera

Gerbera is a flower that is available in different colours. The different varieties of Gerberas carry different meanings, such as innocence, purity, cheerfulness, etc.

Carnation

Carnation flowers are also commonly preferred by florists to create a bouquet. These special flowers, which behold the emotions of love, affection, and gratitude are good to bind and wonderstruck someone you love.

Lily

Calla lilies happen to be the most exotic one of the whole lot. Needless to say, lilies are one of the most commonly desired flowers in the world. If you want to show the passion you can go with a bouquet of orange lilies
